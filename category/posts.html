Factor Models¶

Alpha, the market excess return, is a measure of the active return on an investment, the performance of that investment compared with a suitable market index. An alpha of 1% means the investment's return on investment over a selected period of time was 1% better than the market during that same period. In CAPM model, it's the intercept of sercurity characteristic line (SCL): $$SCL: R_{i,t} - R_f = \alpha_i + \beta_i(R_{M,t} - R_f) + \epsilon_{i,t}$$
